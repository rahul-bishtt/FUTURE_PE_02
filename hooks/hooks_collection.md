# 🪝 Hooks Collection — GlowLab Vitamin C Face Serum
**Platform:** Instagram Reels
**Purpose:** Stop the scroll in the first 2–3 seconds

---

## What is a Hook?
A hook is the very first line spoken or shown in a UGC ad. It must immediately grab attention before the viewer swipes away. The best hooks are:
- Specific (not generic)
- Relatable or surprising
- Make the viewer feel "this is about me"

---

## Hook Type 1 — Pain Point Hooks
*(Target: People frustrated with their skin)*

**Hook 1:**
> *"I tried 6 different serums for my pigmentation. None of them worked. Until this one."*

**Hook 2:**
> *"My dark spots were so stubborn I genuinely thought nothing would ever fix them."*

**Hook 3:**
> *"Dull skin, uneven tone, dark spots — I had all three. Here's what finally helped."*

---

## Hook Type 2 — Question Hooks
*(Target: Curious viewers who stop to find the answer)*

**Hook 4:**
> *"Why does everyone in my college suddenly have glowing skin? I finally found out."*

**Hook 5:**
> *"What if I told you the solution to your dark spots costs less than your weekly Swiggy order?"*

**Hook 6:**
> *"Have you ever wondered why K-beauty serums work so well? It's one ingredient. And I found it in India."*

---

## Hook Type 3 — Shock / Stat Hooks
*(Target: Viewers who respond to numbers and facts)*

**Hook 7:**
> *"Dermatologists say this one ingredient fixes 90% of common skin concerns. Most people still don't use it."*

**Hook 8:**
> *"I spent over ₹8,000 on branded serums last year. Then I found GlowLab for ₹699 and I'm never going back."*

**Hook 9:**
> *"In 4 weeks, my skin looked more even than it had in 4 years. No filter. No edits."*

---

## Hook Type 4 — Relatable / POV Hooks
*(Target: Younger audience, casual scrollers)*

**Hook 10:**
> *"POV: You finally found a serum that actually works under the Indian sun ☀️"*

**Hook 11:**
> *"The serum my mom called 'too cheap to work' gave me the best skin of my life."*

**Hook 12:**

| Hook Type | Best For | Expected Stop Rate |

| Question | All ages | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| Shock/Stat | Skeptical viewers | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| Relatable/POV | 18–25 age group | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |

---

## Key Hook Writing Rules Applied

1. **Be specific** — "₹8,000 on serums" beats "spent a lot of money"
2. **Use "I"** — UGC feels personal, not corporate
3. **Create a gap** — tease the answer, don't reveal it in the hook
4. **Indian context** — Swiggy, Indian sun, college references make it local and relatable
5. **Short sentences** — hooks should be punchy, under 20 words ideally
